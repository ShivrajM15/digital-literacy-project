# Social Media Checklist

## DO's

- **Be respectful** — communicate professionally in posts and comments  
- **Verify information** — check sources before sharing anything  
- **Use privacy settings** — control who can view your content  
- **Build your profile** — share achievements and learning experiences  
- **Think before posting** — assume everything is permanent  

---

## DON'Ts

- **Do not share personal data** — phone numbers, address, financial info  
- **Avoid offensive content** — abusive, discriminatory, or inappropriate posts  
- **Do not spread misinformation** — avoid unverified news or rumors  
- **Avoid arguments online** — do not engage in conflicts or trolling  
- **Do not leak confidential info** — academic or institutional data
