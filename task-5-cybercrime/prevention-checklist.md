### Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

#### General Safety

* Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone
* Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
* Verify website URLs before entering sensitive information
* Avoid downloading apps from untrusted sources
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Be cautious of messages creating urgency or fear

#### UPI / Financial Safety

* Remember: entering your UPI PIN is ONLY for sending money, not receiving
* Always verify “Pay” vs “Collect Request” before approving any transaction
* Do not trust payment screenshots — check your app balance instead
* Use official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm only

#### Social Media & Identity Safety

* Do not overshare personal details (phone number, address, college ID)
* Adjust privacy settings on social media accounts
* Be cautious of fake profiles and impersonation

#### Reporting Cybercrime

* Report incidents immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* Call Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930** (24x7)

Acting quickly increases the chances of recovering lost money.
